Starry Night 2.0

The Starry Night is an oil painting by Vincent van Gogh that depicts a night sky with stars, a moon, and a small town. The painting is known for its swirling brushstrokes, intense colors, and dreamlike quality. I think this is a very good painting, so I am calling it Starry Night 2.0.
